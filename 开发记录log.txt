【*】 系统 STM32F4XX

A.型号：
    STM32F407ZET6

B.晶振：
    - 外部高速晶振：HSE : 20MHZ
    - RTC晶振：32.768KHz
    - 注意：
        用户需要根据外部实际的时钟来配置系统时钟树。
        具体配置在：文件system_stm32f4xx.C文件中
        *=============================================================================================
        *                     STM32F40xxx/41xxx                   |    STM32F429_439XX
        *---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        *        System Clock source          | PLL (HSE)         |      PLL (HSE)
        *---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        *        SYSCLK(Hz)                   | 168000000         |     180000000
        *---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        *        HCLK(Hz)                     | 168000000         |     180000000
        *---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        *        AHB Prescaler                | 1                 |      1
        *---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        *        APB1 Prescaler               | 4                 |     4
        *---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        *        APB2 Prescaler               | 2                 |     2
        *---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        *        HSE Frequency(Hz)            | 20000000          |     25000000
        *---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        *        PLL_M                        | 20                |     25
        *---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        *        PLL_N                        | 336               |     360
        *---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        *        PLL_P                        | 2                 |     2
        *---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        *        PLL_Q                        | 7                 |     7
        *---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

C.各总线运行时钟：
    -系统时钟 = SYCCLK = AHB1 = 168MHZ

    - HCLK = SYSCLK / 1   =  APB_CLK
    - PCLK2 = HCLK / 2    = APB2_CLK    =  84MHZ
    - PCLK1 = HCLK / 4    = APB1_CLK    =  42MHZ
